Pantry Pal

Your AI-powered kitchen assistant that reduces food waste

Role: Full-stack Developer, UX Designer
Technologies: React Native, TensorFlow Lite, Node.js, MongoDB

Project Overview

During my journey into tech, I wanted to create projects that solve real problems I face daily. Pantry Pal was born from watching ingredients go to waste in my pantry. The solution? An AI-powered app that generates recipes from available ingredients.

The app evolved into a comprehensive kitchen assistant that:

  • Scans barcodes to track pantry items
  • Suggests recipes based on expiring ingredients
  • Learns your dietary preferences over time
  • Works offline with 85% on-device AI accuracy

The Mobile Experience

Designed as a mobile-first experience, Pantry Pal helps users:

  • Scan grocery items directly in-store
  • Get recipe suggestions while cooking
  • Create shopping lists based on meal plans
  • Track food waste reduction metrics

The interface uses intuitive gestures and clear visual hierarchy to make kitchen management effortless, even with messy hands during cooking.
